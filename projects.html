PROJECTS

TAKE A LOOK AT SOME OF MY COMPLEATED AND ON GOING PROJECTS.

Logos

Here are some logos, that I am proud of, which helped bring value to the respective brand:

FixIt

An augmented reality solution for repair and maintenance of campus facilities. Asset management systems can be complicated to use and bigger campuses can miss many issues especially if maintenance staff-size isn’t sufficient. FixIt harnesses the power of the student community along with other staff members to efficiently identify and report maintenance issues.

Walk With Me

Walk with me was a submission for uOttaHack where our goal was to solve a transportation issue by creating an app where students could request walking buddies when not comfortable walking home alone. The simple design proved that it could clearly be used anytime and the main safety feature an S.O.S. call was always accessible.

Wish List

An application that allows you to see a friend or a family members' wish list, anonymously, to get them the best gift. My role, on this project, was design lead. The whole team pitched in ideas as we narrowed down to the best design. I was responsible of creating a high fidelity prototype using Sketch & Android Studio. Other members worked on back-end and other functional tasks.

Crescendo

Crescendo is my podcasting platform idea and 2nd place winner at the uOttawa Startup Weekend (2017). It brings together traditional podcast listening and contemporary social media interaction. I designed the interface of the application and user functionality for playing podcasts, profile creation and sharing podcast snippets.

VaciRec

First HTML5/CSS3 project I have designed: a website that keeps track of vaccines, much like an immunization record for all family members.
